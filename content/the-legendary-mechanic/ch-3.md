Using the keycard, Han Xiao bypassed the primary laboratory door and sprinted down the stark, metallic corridors of the Germinal Base. Alarms blared, painting the hallways in flashing crimson light.

*Footsteps. Heavy ones. Approaching fast,* his honed instincts warned him. 

He ducked into an adjacent supply closet just as a squad of heavily armed security personnel ran past. Breathing heavily in the dark, he accessed his interface.

**[Quest Triggered: Escape the Germinal Base]**
**[Objective: Survive and exit the facility.]**
**[Reward: 1000 EXP, Basic Mechanic Knowledge (Blueprint)]**

He needed a weapon. This supply closet was mostly filled with cleaning chemicals and spare parts for the base's ventilation system. But to a Mechanic, 'spare parts' were better than gold.

***

He grabbed a heavy wrench, some copper wiring, and a dismantled battery pack. His hands moved with a practiced blur, a muscle memory retained from countless hours of crafting in his past life.

**[You have successfully crafted: Jury-rigged Stun Baton (Poor Quality)]**

It wasn't much, but the crude device hummed with a low voltage charge. It would be enough to drop a guard if he hit them in a vulnerable spot. 

Stepping out of the closet, Han Xiao gripped his makeshift weapon. The true game had just begun, and he wasn't about to play by the rules.
