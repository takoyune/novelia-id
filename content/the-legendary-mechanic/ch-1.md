Han Xiao opened his eyes heavily, his head throbbing with a dull ache. The air smelled of ozone and cheap antiseptic.

He tried to move, but cold metal restraints held his wrists tight against a slanted examination table. Blinking against the harsh fluorescent lights, his vision finally cleared, revealing a sterile, white-tiled laboratory room. Men in white coats and armed guards in black tactical gear moved about the space.

*Where... where am I?* he thought, panic starting to set in. *Wasn't I just playing Galaxy? I was in the middle of a level 200 boss raid!*

Suddenly, a mechanical voice chimed in his mind.

***

**[System Initializing...]**
**[Welcome to Galaxy!]**
**[Name: Subject Zero]**
**[Race: Carbon-based Human (Base)]**
**[Class: Novice Mechanic]**

***

Han Xiao's eyes widened. A virtual interface, translucent and glowing with faint blue light, hovered in his field of vision. It was the exact UI from his favorite virtual reality MMORPG, *Galaxy*.

But something was terribly wrong. He wasn't a player anymore. The interface clearly labeled his status as an 'NPC'. And the location... Germinal Organization Base, Planet Aquamarine.

"Subject Zero is awake," one of the researchers noted, glancing at a clipboard. "Prepare the memory wiping serum for the final phase."

Memory wiping? Germinal Organization? Han Xiao's heart hammered against his ribs. He knew this lore. The Germinal Organization was the villainous faction of the beginner planet, Aquamarine. If they wiped his memory, he'd become a mindless supersoldier puppet.

He had to escape. And fast.
