The memory wiping serum was scheduled for administration in exactly five minutes. Han Xiao could see the countdown timer ticking away on the researcher's terminal.

He desperately scanned his surroundings. As a top-tier player in his past life, he knew the mechanics of this game inside and out. He just needed an opening.

His eyes fell on a small, motorized cart carrying surgical tools next to his table. It was connected to a power outlet via a frayed cord.

*Mechanic class... I just need to touch it,* he thought.

Straining against his restraints, he managed to inch his right foot just enough to hook the toe of his hospital gown boot onto the cord. With a sharp tug, he yanked the cord from the wall. Sparks flew as the frayed wires shorted out against the metal frame of the cart.

***

The short circuit caused a momentary power surge in the room. The lights flickered, and more importantly, the magnetic locks on his restraints lost power for a fraction of a second.

It was enough.

Han Xiao ripped his arms free, ignoring the agonizing scrape of metal against his skin. Before the guards could react, he threw himself off the table, tackling the nearest researcher and grabbing the man's keycard.

"Subject is loose! Restrain him!" yelled the head scientist.

The guards raised their stun batons, but Han Xiao was already moving with a desperate agility. He didn't have high stats yet, but he had the combat experience of a veteran player.
