## Corporate Restructuring

The merger was complete. The hostile takeover had been a bloodbath of severed contracts and ruined careers, but Arthur Pendelton had secured the majority share. He stood at the penthouse window, looking down at the city lights, swirling a glass of expensive scotch.

He was the king of the corporate jungle.

Then, the glass slipped from his fingers, shattering on the hardwood floor. A sharp, tearing pain ripped through his chest. Myocardial infarction. A massive heart attack. The irony was not lost on him as darkness clawed at the edges of his vision. He had finally won, only to lose it all to a faulty valve.

*So this is bankruptcy,* he thought, as the world faded to black.
