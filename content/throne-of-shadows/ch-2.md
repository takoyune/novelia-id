## Asset Acquisition

The speaker was an imp, a grotesque creature with leathery wings and a twisted, fanged visage. It bowed low, trembling. Arthur recognized it from the memories flooding his new brain. It was a low-level servant of the House of Vane.

His new name was Malakor. He was the last surviving heir of a demonic noble house that had been eradicated by the Holy Paladins of the surface world.

"Report," Arthur—now Malakor—demanded, his voice cold and commanding.

The imp flinched. "The Paladins have breached the upper levels of the catacombs, Young Master. They are purging the remnants. We must flee deeper into the Abyss!"

Malakor accessed his new System interface. 

**[Host: Malakor Vane]**
**[Level: 1]**
**[Class: Soul Devourer]**
**[Skills: Inspect (Lv.1), Consume (Lv.1)]**

Fleeing was bad for business. It projected weakness.

***

"We don't flee," Malakor stated, standing up. His body was lean, his skin a pale, sickly gray, and small horns curled from his brow. "We restructure. How many guards are in the adjacent corridor?"

"T-two, Young Master. But they are holy warriors! You are too weak!"

Malakor ignored the imp. He picked up a rusted iron spike from the ground. It wasn't a corporate pen, but it would have to do for signing his first termination notice in this new world.

He crept toward the corridor entrance, his steps unnaturally silent. Two figures in gleaming silver armor stood guard, their holy auras illuminating the gloom. 

Time for a hostile takeover.
