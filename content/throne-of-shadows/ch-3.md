## Hostile Takeover

Malakor observed the two Paladins from the shadows. They were relaxed, confident in their holy armor and the perceived weakness of the dungeon denizens. A fatal error in risk assessment.

He activated **[Inspect]**.

**[Target: Paladin Initiate]**
**[Level: 5]**
**[Threat Level: High]**

They outleveled him significantly. A direct confrontation was out of the question. He needed leverage.

He picked up a loose stone and tossed it down a side passage. The clatter echoed loudly in the quiet catacombs.

"What was that?" one Paladin muttered, gripping his sword. "Stay here. I'll check it out."

The classic mistake. Never split your forces.

***

As the first Paladin moved away, Malakor waited until he was out of sight. Then, he lunged at the remaining guard. He didn't aim for the heavy armor; he aimed for the vulnerable joint at the back of the knee.

He drove the rusted spike in with brutal efficiency. The Paladin screamed, collapsing to one knee. 

Before the man could recover or call out a warning, Malakor clamped his hand over the Paladin's face and activated his innate skill.

**[Consume]**

A vortex of dark energy erupted from Malakor's palm. The Paladin's screams were muffled as his very life force—his soul—was forcibly extracted and drawn into Malakor's body.

The sensation was intoxicating. It was better than closing a billion-dollar deal.

**[Enemy Consumed. +500 EXP. Level Up!]**
**[Acquired Skill: Lesser Healing Magic (Corrupted)]**

Malakor let the empty husk of armor fall to the floor. He turned toward the dark passage where the second Paladin had gone, a terrifying, predatory smile spreading across his face. 

Business was booming.
